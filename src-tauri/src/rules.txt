! EasyPrivacy essential tracker rules — bundled with sup
!
! IMPORTANT SAFETY NOTE:
! This list must ONLY contain genuine third-party trackers that WhatsApp Web
! has no operational dependency on. Do NOT block any Meta/Facebook infrastructure
! (graph.facebook.com, connect.facebook.net, etc.) because:
!   1. WhatsApp Web uses Meta infrastructure for authentication and session
!      management. Blocking it can break functionality.
!   2. Sudden cessation of Meta telemetry from a "browser" can trigger
!      anomaly detection and flag the session as a bot/unofficial client.
!
! The adblock engine runs in the Rust process and intercepts at the network
! protocol level (via custom URI scheme handlers), not inside the WebView.

! === Google Analytics ===
||google-analytics.com^$third-party
||analytics.google.com^$third-party
||ssl.google-analytics.com^$third-party
||stats.g.doubleclick.net^$third-party

! === Google Ads (not needed by WhatsApp Web) ===
||adservice.google.com^
||pagead2.googlesyndication.com^

! === Scorecard / Nielsen ===
||scorecardresearch.com^$third-party
||pixel.quantserve.com^$third-party

! === Amplitude / Heap / Mixpanel (pure analytics, not used by WhatsApp Web) ===
||cdn.heapanalytics.com^
||cdn.amplitude.com^$third-party
||api.amplitude.com^$third-party
||api.mixpanel.com^$third-party
||cdn.mxpnl.com^$third-party

! === Segment (third-party analytics) ===
||cdn.segment.com^$third-party
||api.segment.io^$third-party

! NOTE: We intentionally do NOT block:
!   - graph.facebook.com     (WhatsApp auth/session)
!   - connect.facebook.net   (WhatsApp Web scripts)
!   - *.whatsapp.com         (obviously)
!   - *.whatsapp.net         (WhatsApp media/CDN)
!   - *.fbcdn.net            (Facebook CDN used by WhatsApp)
!   - crashlytics.com        (WhatsApp uses this for crash reporting)
!   - sentry.io              (may be used by WhatsApp Web)
